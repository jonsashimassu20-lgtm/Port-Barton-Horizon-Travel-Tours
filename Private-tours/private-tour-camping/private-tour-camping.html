This Tour A goes by the name of THE MUST-SEE. It brings together the most spectacular coastal highlights in Port Barton. Through this trip, you can look forward to thrilling experiences both by exploring colorful underwater habitats and participating in shore adventures.

Program and Itinerary

This full-day trip runs for roughly 8 hours. You will see the top highlights and planned activities for your adventure outlined below.

1st Spot Day Tour

9:00 AM

Twin Reef: Dive into Port Barton's premier underwater paradise. Swim through a vibrant, thriving coral system teeming with exotic marine life and multicolored tropical fish.

2nd Spot Day Tour

10:00 AM

Turtle Spot: Enjoy a thrilling and magical encounter in the open sea. Swim alongside majestic sea turtles as they glide through their pristine, natural habitat.

3rd Spot Day Tour

11:30 AM

Inaladelan Island: Relax on the shore while your boat crew prepares a fresh buffet lunch. Unwind on the beach, snap photos, or check out the island's protected turtle nesting sanctuary.

4th Spot Day Tour

2:00 PM

Aquarium Reef: Explore a second incredible diving location that mirrors a real-life aquarium. Discover massive coral formations and swim with massive schools of curious reef fish.

5th Spot Day Tour

3:00 PM

Exotic Island: Walk across the shallow sandbar to the adjacent island during low tide. Refresh yourself by lounging under the shade with a freshly cracked, cold coconut juice.

6th Spot Day Tour

3:30 PM

Starfish Island: Wade through crystal-clear sandbars crowded with beautiful, colorful starfishes. This shallow spot is perfect for swimming, relaxing, and taking postcard-worthy photos.

Go Back To Mainland

3:30 PM

Camp Preparation: Return to the mainland briefly to stock up on personal supplies, snacks, or alcoholic beverages for the night. Select your preferred island destination to set up your overnight camping tents.

Island Camping

6:00 PM

Overnight Stay: Settle into your island campsite as the crew grills a fresh dinner. Spend the evening enjoying a cozy beach bonfire, stargazing, or taking a peaceful night swim.

Go Back to Mainland

6:00 AM

Morning Departure: Catch a stunning island sunrise and enjoy a hearty breakfast by the shore. Pack your gear for an early morning boat ride back to the Port Barton mainland.
