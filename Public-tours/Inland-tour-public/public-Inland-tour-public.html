Description

Port Barton
Around 8 hours
Public Tour

Categories: Boat Tour, Island Hopping

Experience the absolute best of both worlds with an itinerary that beautifully balances thriving marine sanctuaries and refreshing inland wonders. This journey takes you from snorkeling over the sprawling coral structures of Lagoon Reef, Wide Reef, and Capsalay Reef, straight to the iconic sands of Giligans Beach and Capsalay Island. To top it all off, you’ll head mainland for a scenic trek to the refreshing jungle waters of Bigaho Falls.

Program and Itinerary

This full-day trip runs for roughly 8 hours. You will see the top highlights and planned activities for your adventure outlined below.

Departure

8:45 AM

Our professional tour guide will meet you directly at your hotel lobby, or you can comfortably gather with us at our main office located at Alex Coffee house.

Bigaho Waterfalls

9:00 - 9:30 AM

We will kick off the day’s adventure at our first mainland destination: the breathtaking Bigaho Waterfalls. Take a quick, rewarding jungle walk to reach its refreshing freshwater pool.

Long Beach, San Vicente

10:30 - 11:30 AM

Next, we head to the iconic Long Beach in San Vicente, known as the longest continuous white-sand beach in the Philippines. This spectacular 14.7km stretch of powdery sand is beautifully lined with endless coconut trees and native nipa huts.

Lunch

12:30 - 1:30 PM

Take a break and enjoy a delicious freshly cooked lunch. We serve tasty local dishes along with seafood selections, prepared based on the best fresh catches available at the morning market.

Alimanguhan Surfing Spot

1:30 - 2:30 PM

Our journey continues to the scenic shores of Alimanguhan Surfing Spot. Enjoy the refreshing sea breeze, watch local surfers catch the waves, or snap photos of the beautiful coast.

NaoNao Beach or Pamuayan Beach

2:30 - 4:00 PM

We move forward to our fourth destination, unwinding on the tranquil shores of either NaoNao Beach or Pamuayan Beach. This stop offers the perfect quiet pocket of sand to swim, stroll, or completely relax away from the crowds.

Sunset & Complimentary Drinks

4:30 - 5:30 PM

We finish our tour back at the main beachfront of Port Barton. Unwind on the shore with a cold complimentary drink of your choice as you watch the spectacular sunset.
