Description

Categories: Boat Tour, Island Hopping

This Tour A goes by the name of THE MUST-SEE. It brings together the most spectacular coastal highlights in Port Barton. Through this trip, you can look forward to thrilling experiences both by exploring colorful underwater habitats and participating in shore adventures.

Program and Itinerary

This full-day trip runs for roughly 8 hours. You will see the top highlights and planned activities for your adventure outlined below.

Meeting At Alex Coffee House

8:30 AM

Our meeting point is at our office in Tiu Homestay. We’ll move to the beach for the departure which will happen at 9:00AM.

Twin Reef

9:30 AM

We’ll stop at this first destination where you can snorkel and dive, and see more corals reefs and more marine species.

Turtle Spot

10:30 AM

We’ll stop at the turtles spot where you’ll be able to swim, snorkel and take adventures with the sea turtles.

Exotic Island

11:30 AM

We’ll stop at this destination where you’ll be able to swim, snorkel, have lunch, lay down at the beach under the sun, do sports if you want too and more.

Fantastic Reef

1:00 PM

We’ll stop on this spot where you’ll be able to swim and see different coral reef building and other marine species.

Paradise Island

2:30 PM

We’ll stop at Paradise island where you can swim, do sports. This is also an alternate spot for taking the lunch depending on the wishes of the guest and the crowds. You can also decide to explore a bit the island by foot.

Starfish Island

3:30 PM

We’ll stop at Starfish island where you’ll be able to swim and have fun with the starfishes. If you are lucky, we can also include the sandbar as a bonus destination for your tour!
