We offer convenient vehicle collection and return services exclusively around Port Barton proper. 🛵

Leave a clear photocopy of your passport accompanied by a refundable cash security bond of ₱10,000. This cash deposit will be handed back to you in full once the items are returned undamaged.

All renters are required to review and sign our standard rental agreement upon receiving the equipment. This package includes a mandatory liability policy that covers specific third-party liabilities up to ₱100,000. Our rentals come with absolute driving freedom as we do not impose any mileage restrictions or distance limits during your booking. You are welcome to explore as far as you want around the island without worrying about hidden tracking fees or extra per-kilometer charges.

Fuel costs are entirely separate from the baseline rental rate. Your motorcycle will be handed over with a completely full tank of gasoline at the start of your booking. We kindly ask that you fill up the tank right before drop-off so the vehicle is returned with that exact same level of fuel.
